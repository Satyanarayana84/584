understanding Lists in HTML

in HTML,Lists are used to group related items.There are three main types of lists: unordered Lists,ordered lists,and defination lists.Lists can also be nested with in each other.

unordered List (Bulleted List)

An unordered list is a list where the order of the items doesn't matter.It is usually displayed with bullet points:

  • item1
  • item2
  • item3
  • item4
    1. Ordered Sub-item1
    2. Ordered Sub-item2
    3. Ordered Sub-item3

Ordered List (Numbered List)

An Ordered list is where the ordere of the items matters.It is usually displaye with numbebr or letters.

  1. first item
